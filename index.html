About me

I'm a sixth year Ph.D. student in Department of Electrical Engineering, University of Washington, Seattle with Prof. Maryam Fazel. I graduated as Bachelor of Engineering from Department of Electronics Engineering, Tsinghua University, China. I'm interested in statistical machine learning, optimization and signal processing. Currently I'm working on first order algorithms solving optimization problem of non-convex function, and regularized control/reinforcement learning problems.

I joined Google in June-September, 2019, on online optimization algorithm applied in video coding. I joined Nokia Bell Labs in June-August, 2021, on channel estimation for audio echo cancellation.
